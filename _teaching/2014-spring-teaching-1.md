Teaching and Outreach
======
* August 2020 - Present: QCB Virtual Open House Organizer, Princeton University
  * Organized the first (and now annual) virtual QCB open house with a team of graduate students
  * Created to recruit applicants from non-traditional and under-represented backgrounds
  * Set up a faculty panel and graduate student discussion room for participants’ questions

* October 2020 - Present: Access, Diversity and Inclusion Outreach Student Ambassador, Princeton University
  * Recruited students from under-represented backgrounds with the ADI team at Princeton
  * Annual participant in the Ivy + Graduate School Fair to recruit prospective students from Puerto Rico
  * Annual panelist in the Prospective PhD Preview – the university-wide diversity and inclusion recruitment event

* January 2022 - June 2022: Assistant in Instruction, Princeton University
  * TA for the class Theoretical Ecology taught by Professor Simon Levin
  * Wrote and graded homeworks, quizzes and exams
  * Planned and lead two weekly precepts

* May 2020 - June 2021: Tutor in the Princeton Online Tutoring Network, Princeton University
  * Provided free tutoring assistance to under-served K-12 students in the local community
  * Tutored middle and high school math students virtually during the COVID-19 pandemic
  * Created lesson plans and curricula for Algebra 1 and 2
